Benefits of Bodybuilding

Some people claim that there are no unique benefits to bodybuilding training –aiming to increase muscle mass and decrease fat mass. They aver that bodybuilding is a waste of time because it (supposedly) only improves your appearance, not your performance. These people claim that one should do so-called "functional" training instead. According to these people, calisthenics, Cross Fit, powerlifting, Olympic lifting, and gymnastics produce a more "functional" fitness than bodybuilding-style training. They maintain that if there are any benefits of bodybuilding, they can be more easily acquired, along with a more functional fitness, through these "functional" training methods.
